Privacy Policy for GoalFlow

Last updated: November 21, 2024

Privacy Policy Overview

GoalFlow ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

Our extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your daily and monthly goals
  • Goal completion status
  • Goal priorities
  • Language preferences

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your information with third parties. When you choose to share your goals summary via WhatsApp, you control what information is shared and with whom.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and is subject to Chrome's built-in security measures. We do not have access to your stored data.

User Rights

You can:

  • View all your stored data within the extension
  • Delete your data at any time by clearing the extension's storage
  • Change your language preferences
  • Control what information you share via WhatsApp

Changes to The Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Chrome Web Store.
